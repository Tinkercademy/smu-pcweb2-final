Mission

We love our dads, husbands, boyfriends and brothers, but that doesn't mean we want to wear their shoes! Working as an executive in the transportation industry, I could never find the right footwear for my job. The safety boots either didn't fit, or looked ridiculous with my clothes. Together with my team, we made it our mission to create Juno Jones, and to not only bring women options in beautiful, high performance work boots, but to help normalize and empower women in nontraditional fields.
